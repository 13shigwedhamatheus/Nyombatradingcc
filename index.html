Understanding Our Business

Nyomba Garage provides reliable vehicle repair and tyre services to the local community. We aim to offer fast, affordable and professional automotive services to keep vehicles safe on the road.

The business serves private car owners, taxis, farmers and business in the area .

Core services

Tyer Services:

  • Tyre fitting and balancing
  • Wheel alignment
  • Tyre repair and replacement
  • Tube repair
  • Selling new & used tyers

Mechanical Services:

  • Oil changes
  • Engine checks
  • Brake inspection
  • Basic vehicle check
  • Battery replacement
